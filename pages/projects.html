PacMan

Dive into this modern rendition of the classic Pac-Man game, masterfully crafted using JavaScript and the p5.js library. Embedded within an HTML framework, it's playable on any web browser.

The game boasts a detailed 2D grid arena, with the iconic A* path-finding technique steering ghost movements. Delight in the familiar chomping sounds and chase mechanics that evoke nostalgia.

It's the quintessential Pac-Man experience, reimagined for the contemporary web, ensuring hours of fun and challenge.

Sudoku

Immerse yourself in this captivating Sudoku game, meticulously crafted with JavaScript and the p5.js library. Engage with the traditional 9x9 Sudoku grid on an HTML canvas, with a diverse array of puzzles available at the click of a button.

Rock Paper Scissor Simulator

Step into a dynamic simulation of the classic Rock-Paper-Scissors game, innovatively implemented using p5.js. Entities traverse the canvas, engaging and interacting based on the timeless game rules.

They consume, reproduce, and evolve based on user-configurable parameters, providing a unique twist on the traditional gameplay.

Enhanced with vibrant imagery and interactivity, this project offers a refreshing digital take on an age-old game, blending entertainment with a hint of educational value.
